My Top Four Personal Values

Hard Work

Why is this important to you?

Hard work is important to me because it is important to try your hardest in everything you do, because you never know what results may occur.

How did you develop this value?

I developed this value from playing soccer and feeling guilty if I didn't try hard enough and my team suffered because of it.

Accountability

Why is this important to you?

Accountability is important to me because people need to be able to trust you in every circumstance.

How did you develop this value?

I developed this value from my parents holding me accountable in school.

Integrity

Why is this important to you?

Integrity is important to me because you should always be doing what you are supposed to be doing no matter if or who is watching you.

How did you develop this value?

I develpoped this value in elementary school when my teachers would leave the classroom and expect us to show integrity

Discipline

Why is this important to you?

It is important to stay disciplined because it is one of the only ways to prove to yourself that you can grow and improve.

How did you develop this value?

I developed this value while doing homework, practicing soccer, and working out.
